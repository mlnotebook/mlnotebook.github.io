Royal Society Summer Science 2017 more_vert

The Royal Society, London

Royal Society Summer Science 2017close

The Royal Society Summer Science (RSSS) exhibition takes parts in the grounds of the Royal Society, in London. It is one of the most well-known and well-respected public engagement events in the scientific community. Exhibitors from across the country, from academic institutions and industry, demonstrate their cutting-edge research to the public across 7 days. It is an opportunity to engage people of all ages with Science.

This year our stand was all about the heart. 3D-printed models of healthy and diseased hearts were shown alongside their corresponding MRI scans. A gyroscopic 3D model demonstrated resynchronisation therapy and the star of the event, where you could hold your "Heart in Your Hands"
